SLIDE 2: THE PROBLEM (The "why")
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[Visual]: Split screen. Left side: A student frustrated with a "Suggestion Box". Right side: An admin buried under paperwork/emails.
[Text]: 
    - Information Overload: Admins receive hundreds of duplicate or vague complaints.
    - Delayed Resolution: Critical safety issues get buried under trivial complaints.
    - Student Voicelessness: Students feel unheard and stop reporting issues.
    - Privacy Concerns: Fear of backlash prevents honest feedback.

[Speaker Notes]:
"The current way of handling campus issues is broken. Admins are overwhelmed by unorganized emails, and critical issues—like a broken lab equipment or safety hazard—often get missed. Students stop reporting because they feel ignored or fear for their privacy."

SLIDE 3: THE SOLUTION - CampusPulse AI
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[Visual]: A high-level diagram showing: Student -> AI Brain (Gemini) -> Smart Dashboard -> Admin.
[Text]: 
    - Automated Analysis: Every complaint is read by AI instantly.
    - Smart Prioritization: AI detects Urgency (High/Medium/Low) automatically.
    - Actionable Insights: Suggests immediate solutions to admins.
    - Privacy First: Anonymous Reporting with Username protection.

[Speaker Notes]:
"CampusPulse AI acts as an intelligent layer between students and administration. It doesn't just store complaints; it understands them. It filters noise, highlights urgency, and protects student identity."

SLIDE 4: POWERED BY GOOGLE TECHNOLOGIES (The "How")
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[Visual]: Logos of the tech stack.
[Text]: 
    1. Google Gemini API (Model 1.5/2.0): The Brain. Handles Sentiment Analysis, Summarization, and Categorization.
    2. Firebase Firestore: The Database. Real-time syncing for live admin dashboards.
    3. Firebase Authentication: Secure login for Students and Admins.
    4. React + Tailwind CSS: Modern, responsive "Coffee Shop" UI.

[Speaker Notes]:
"We leveraged the best of Google's ecosystem. We use the Gemini API to analyze text in real-time, categorizing complex complaints into clear data points. Firebase ensures that as soon as a student hits 'Submit', the admin sees it instantly."
